PCA Rally

Starting from point A and getting to point B is all this event looks to be. It is so much more, along with a series of questions guiding the driver and navigator to their destination traversing the beautiful fall folliage creating a wonderful array of autum colors which are imaculate. There is really nothing like it, the trees decide to show off their artsy side this time of the year and their color palate change brings such a unique life to the forest. It allows a certain serenity to be achieved while just driving and enjoying the world we live in.
